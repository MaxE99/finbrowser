However, that has changed in the last decade and now the open web has become home to many experts freely sharing their investing knowledge with the world. With FinBrowser, you can find and manage these sources.

Core Features

1

Find Great Stock Market Sources

StockBrowser gives you an overview of great sources from all over the web. From Substack, Fintwit to YouTube, you can find them all here.

2

Stay Up To Date On Your Favorite Sources

Stay up to date on the the activity of your favorite stock market sources and get notified when there is new content.

3

Get To Know More About A Specific Sector

Interested in a specific stock market sector like tech? Here you will find the top articles and experts on the topic that will help you to become an expert in the industry yourself.

4

Find Articles About Specific Stock

Want to know more about a specific stock? Find out what the best sources on the open web have to say about it.

5

Create And Follow Lists

Create lists to collect your research in one place and see what research other users have done.

6

Create A Feed To Manage The Information Flow

Manage access to your research and the authors and lists you follow in one easy-to-access place.
